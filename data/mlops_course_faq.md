## Exam Structure
- **Presentation of Seminar Topic (40%):** Sessions 3 & 4. Students provide an overview of challenges and existing approaches related to their chosen seminar topic, followed by Q&A.
- **Demo Presentation (40%):** Sessions 5 & 6. Students introduce at least one tool relevant to their topic with prepared examples showcasing core features.
- **Peer Review (20%):** Students not presenting complete a brief peer-review task evaluating assigned presentations with constructive, topic-focused feedback.

## MLOps Concepts
- **MLOps** is a set of practices at the intersection of Machine Learning, Data Engineering and DevOps with the goal to deploy and maintain ML-enabled systems in production efficiently and effectively.
- **AI Engineering** is the systematic application of tools, methods, and processes to develop high-quality ML systems.
- The ML model lifecycle has four phases: Model Development & Iteration, Prepare for Production, Deployment to Production, and Monitoring & Feedback Loop.
- Machine learning systems are not built once and shipped - they are continuously operated, monitored, and improved.
- The maturity of the ML development process is determined by the level of automation of its Data, Model, and Code pipelines (Manual, ML Pipeline Automation, CI/CD Pipeline Automation).
